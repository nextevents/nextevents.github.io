To attend any EQUIX event you must first apply as a guest or member.

 

If you are accepted, we will respond with the payment method to pay the corresponding fee.

 

Couples who cannot afford to be members but wish to enjoy some events as guests can join by paying a one off fee.

They will be able to access each event at a basic price and the approved guest category entitles them to participate in contests.

 

Single ladies are welcome under the same category as approved guests.

 

Single gentlemen are invited to join the club if they wish, although some events are reserved for couples and single ladies only.

 

Some events are themed and require a dress code that must be met. 

For all other events all gentlemen are required to wear a proper tuxedo with bow tie.

Ladies shall be elegantly dressed in evening wear or lingerie covered with a coat.
